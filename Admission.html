Admission
Find all the information about our admission here

The selection of students into the college is based on the following criteria:

  • An entrance examination. Candidates will be tested in English Language, Mathematics and General paper
  • Children must be ten (10) years of age to be admitted into Year 7 except in highly exceptional cases.

After administration of the test, successful candidates are offered admission and will be issued an admission letter.

Admission remains provisional until the non-refundable deposit fee, which confirms acceptance of offer is paid. On payment of the deposit, the school issues the prospectus, which gives a more detailed overview of the school including boarding requirements for intending boarders. The school's code of conduct, list of books, and school calendar will be made available on full payment of fees. To ensure proper planning, the school requests that the school fees be paid in full by the deadline. Failure to meet the deadline will lead to forfeiture of reservation to candidates on the waiting list.

For uk enquiries, call 07737599787 or 07738906602

Admission is neither restricted to nor automatic for pupils from Juniors International Primary School (the junior arm of the college).

School Fees

The fee is affordable and expected to be paid termly or annually by stipulated deadlines. All fees should be paid by teller or bank draft to the School Account at the designated Banks. No part payments are allowed in the school. Fees could be paid at any of the Bank's branches nationwide. Provision has also been made for students whose parents are resident abroad and will like to pay at their base. Please find out details from the school. First term fees are usually higher because of Exercise Books, Uniforms, House Wears, Sports Wears, Cardigans, etc that will be supplied.

Resumption

All students both old and new (day and boarders) are expected to report at the office on arrival (on resumption) where the Principal, the House Parents or the Classroom Tutor will formally receive them as the case may be. Any student found in the school without proper clearance and entry permit from the college authorities will be regarded as an offender. Parents of boarders are permitted in the hostel only on the first day of resumption to help the child settle in. No parent will be allowed in school after 6.00 p.m. on such days. No student will also be received after 6.00 p.m. as the school gates will be locked.

Withdrawal

A term's notice in writing is required. In the light of this, a deposit of Twenty Thousand Naira(N20, 000.00) is to be paid when parents come to settle a new intake (whether day or boarding on resumption). The deposit is forfeited if the condition for withdrawal is not adhered to. This is to allow candidates on the waiting list to be informed and have the anticipated space filled up.
